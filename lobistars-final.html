Our Story

Built On Benue Soil

Lobi Stars Football Club was founded in 1969 in Makurdi, the capital of Benue State. For over five decades, the club has stood as the heartbeat of football in North-Central Nigeria, earning a fierce and loyal following.

Playing their home matches at the iconic Aper Aku Stadium, the Red and Gold have competed at the highest levels of Nigerian football, winning the Nigerian Premier Football League title multiple times and representing the nation in continental competitions.

More than a football club — we are a community, a culture, and the enduring pride of Benue State.

Our Heritage

Club History

1969
The Club is Founded
Lobi Stars Football Club is established in Makurdi, Benue State, laying the foundation for decades of football excellence in North-Central Nigeria.
1980s
Rise to National Prominence
The club begins competing at the highest level of Nigerian football, building a passionate following and establishing Makurdi as a serious football city.
1990s
First NPFL Title
Lobi Stars claim their first Nigerian Premier Football League title, cementing their status as one of the elite clubs in Nigerian football.
2000s
Continental Campaigns
The Red and Gold represent Nigeria on the continent, competing in CAF competitions and gaining experience against Africa's best clubs.
2018
League Champions Again
Lobi Stars deliver one of their finest seasons, winning the NPFL and qualifying for the CAF Champions League group stage — a historic achievement.
Today
Building the Future
With renewed investment, a growing academy, and passionate supporters, Lobi Stars are building toward their next golden era.
